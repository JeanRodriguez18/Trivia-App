Welcome to my trivia app

This is a trivia game that combines design elements created with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with data retrieval capabilities enabled by the use of jQuery and the Open Trivia Database API. With a vast database of diverse trivia questions at its disposal, this app challenges players to test their knowledge across a wide range of topics, from history and science to pop culture and sports.
